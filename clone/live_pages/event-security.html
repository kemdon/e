THREAT LANDSCAPE

Deployed across key sectors requiring the highest standards of performance.

Physical Nuisance

During major events, consumer drones take off illegally in surges; pilots adopt “hit-and-run” tactics, switching launch points repeatedly, easily causing crowd panic or collisions with stages / LED screens.

Sensitive Attacks

Covert filming of VIPs’ or athletes’ convoy routes, dropping leaflets / incendiary bottles, jamming event communication-truck signals—posing political and security risks.

Order Disruption

Legally-registered media drones deviate from flight paths and mix with illegal aircraft; mis-identification & mis-countermeasures may interrupt live broadcasts and hinder event support.
